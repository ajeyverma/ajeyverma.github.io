About Me

Hi! I'm Pawan Yadav, a travel enthusiast sharing my experiences exploring the beautiful city of Mysore.

Mysore Palace

The magnificent Mysore Palace is one of India's most famous tourist attractions. The palace's architecture is a blend of Hindu, Muslim, Rajput, and Gothic styles.

Chamundi Hills

Located at a height of 1065 meters, Chamundi Hills is a popular religious and tourist destination offering panoramic views of Mysore city.

My Mysore Experience

Visiting Mysore was a magical experience. The city's rich cultural heritage, delicious food, and friendly people made my trip unforgettable. The famous Mysore Dosa and local sweets are must-tries for any visitor.
